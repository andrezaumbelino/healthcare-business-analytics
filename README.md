## 🧠 Overview

This project simulates a hospital performance analysis for a fictional healthcare provider (**ClariHealth**), focusing on identifying key drivers of **revenue, efficiency, and operational performance**.

---

## 📁 Files


- [Power BI Dashboard (.pbix) link]([(https://github.com/andrezaumbelino/healthcare-business-analytics/blob/main/Hospital%20Performance%20Insights.pbix])
- Dataset (~55k hospital admissions)  
- Technical Documentation (data modeling, DAX, methodology)  
- Executive Analysis (business insights and recommendations)  

---

## ⚙️ Data Model

- Star Schema (Fact: Admissions)  
- Dimensions: Patient, Hospital, Doctor, Disease, Admission Type, Insurance  
- Calendar table for time intelligence  
- Active date: **Discharge Date**

---

## 📊 Key Insights

- Revenue is driven by:
  - **Admission volume**
  - **Operational efficiency (Length of Stay)**  

- ~80% of stays exceed 8 days → major efficiency opportunity  
- Clinical mix has low impact on revenue  
- Best hospitals combine **scale + efficiency**  
- Elective admissions are more efficient and predictable  

---

## 🚀 Recommendations

- Reduce length of stay  
- Increase bed turnover  
- Focus on elective admissions  
- Replicate best-performing hospitals  

---


## 🎯 Key Takeaway

Hospital performance is primarily driven by **operational efficiency**, not by patient mix or volume growth.
